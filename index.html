1. What is Covered
This policy provides weekly income protection for registered gig workers whose work is disrupted by verified environmental and civil events. Payouts are parametric — triggered automatically when environmental data from authorised APIs (IMD, CPCB, NDMA, PIB) crosses defined thresholds. No manual loss assessment is required.

3. Anti-Fraud & TAS Requirements
All claims are subject to Trip Authenticity Score (TAS) verification. A score below 30 results in automatic claim rejection. Scores between 30–69 trigger partial payouts and manual review. GPS data is collected every 4 minutes. Liveness challenges are issued randomly during claimed disruption periods. Any detected spoofing is reported to IRDAI.
4. Premium & Actuarial Framework
Premiums are calculated dynamically using a multi-factor ML model incorporating: city historical risk, operating zone waterlogging history, weather forecasts, platform type, and prior claim history. The pool targets a 62% loss ratio with 28% claims reserve. Excess losses beyond ₹50L per event are covered by IRDAI-approved reinsurance.
