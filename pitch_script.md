# Shramik Sahay - 2 Minute Prototype Pitch Script

**Target Duration**: ~2 minutes (approx. 260 words)
**Pacing**: Conversational, empathetic, and tech-forward.

---

### 🕒 [0:00 - 0:30] The Problem & Introduction
*(Visual: Open on Splash Screen)*

"Hi everyone. We are presenting **Shramik Sahay** — an automated, AI-enabled income protection platform for the gig economy. 

Delivery workers in India often face a 20 to 30 percent income loss due to forced downtime from heavy rain, toxic air quality, or extreme heatwaves. Traditional insurance requires complicated claims and paperwork, which fails them. They need instant, predictable stability."

### 🕒 [0:30 - 1:00] Localization & AI Premium
*(Visual: Click Language Selection -> Registration -> Plan Selection)*

"Our prototype offers a fully localized, inclusive onboarding experience in 5 regional languages. 

Let’s follow Arjun, a full-time rider in Bengaluru. When Arjun registers, our AI engine steps in. Using a logistic regression model, it analyzes historical weather risk, location, and platform earnings to generate a dynamic, affordable weekly premium—just ₹65 for ₹5,000 of coverage. It’s micro-insurance tailored to his reality."

### 🕒 [1:00 - 1:30] The Dashboard & Parametric Triggers
*(Visual: Dashboard Screen -> Scroll to Trigger Simulator)*

"Here is the active dashboard. The system passively monitors 6 environmental triggers via authorized APIs, including IMD for weather and CPCB for air quality. 

Let’s simulate a disruption. We trigger **'Heavy Rainfall (>50mm)'**. 
Because this is parametric insurance, the trigger event is detected automatically. There is no manual loss assessment. The principle is simple: if the disruption happens, the payout happens."

### 🕒 [1:30 - 2:00] Adversarial Defense & Instant Payout
*(Visual: Anti-Spoofing Shield Screen -> Payout Confirmation)*

"But what about fraud? To keep premiums low and sustainable, we built a robust adversarial defense system. 

Before any transaction, our completely automated **Trip Authenticity Score (TAS)** uses a neural network to verify GPS trajectory, speed plausibility, and liveness to instantly block spoofing attacks. 

Arjun’s TAS score is verified as authentic. His partial income replacement is instantly deposited via UPI. Shramik Sahay is transparent, IRDAI-compliant, and protects the backbone of the gig economy. Thank you."
