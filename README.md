Overview

Shramik Sahay is an AI-enabled parametric income protection platform designed for food delivery partners working with platforms such as Zomato, Swiggy, Zepto, and Dunzo.

Food delivery workers are among the most vulnerable participants in the gig economy because their work requires continuous outdoor travel and road mobility. Environmental disruptions such as heavy rainfall, heatwaves, hazardous air pollution, and extreme weather alerts can significantly reduce delivery activity and working hours.

These disruptions often result in 20–30% loss of weekly income, yet gig workers currently lack any structured financial protection mechanism against such external conditions.

Shramik Sahay addresses this gap by providing automated income protection through parametric insurance. Instead of submitting manual claims, payouts are triggered automatically when environmental thresholds are crossed.

The platform integrates:
	•	Environmental monitoring APIs
	•	AI-based income prediction models
	•	GPS activity verification
	•	Automated digital payouts

This ensures fast, transparent, and fair compensation for delivery workers when environmental disruptions affect their earning potential.

Problem Statement

Food delivery gig workers frequently experience income instability caused by environmental disruptions, including:
	•	Heavy rainfall and flooding
	•	Heatwaves and extreme temperatures
	•	Hazardous air pollution levels
	•	Storm warnings and natural disruptions

During such conditions, workers often:
	•	Reduce their working hours
	•	Stop deliveries temporarily
	•	Face unsafe working environments

These factors result in significant income loss.

Currently, gig workers do not have access to insurance products designed specifically for income disruption due to environmental conditions.

Application Workflow

The Shramik Sahay platform operates through the following workflow:
	1.	Worker registers on the Shramik Sahay mobile application
	2.	Worker selects a weekly income protection plan
	3.	Worker provides basic income information
	4.	AI predicts expected weekly earnings
	5.	The platform continuously monitors environmental conditions
	6.	If thresholds are exceeded, a parametric trigger event is activated
	7.	AI estimates income loss
	8.	Automated payout is transferred via UPI or digital wallet

Parametric Triggers

Parametric insurance works by triggering payouts based on objective external data rather than manual claims.

Weather Triggers

Event	Threshold
Heavy Rain	Rainfall > 50 mm
Heatwave	Temperature > 42°C
Storm Alert	Official weather alert issued

Environmental Triggers

Event	Threshold
Severe Air Pollution	AQI > 400
Hazardous Air Quality	AQI > 450

If these thresholds are exceeded within a delivery zone, the system automatically activates the payout process.

GPS-Based Activity Tracking

Shramik Sahay integrates GPS tracking APIs to verify worker activity.

GPS tracking allows the platform to:
	•	Track worker movement during delivery hours
	•	Verify activity during disruption periods
	•	Estimate working hours and delivery distance
	•	Detect abnormal activity patterns to prevent fraud

Combining GPS activity data with environmental triggers ensures payouts occur only when actual work disruption happens.

Fraud Detection

AI-based anomaly detection identifies suspicious patterns such as:
	•	Multiple accounts linked to a single user
	•	Abnormal payout patterns
	•	Workers receiving payouts while remaining fully active
	•	GPS spoofing patterns

This ensures system integrity and fairness.
